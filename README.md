# Water Timer: Visual Hourglass Experience 💧⏱️

**Water Timer** is a calming and intuitive **Visual Countdown Timer** built with **Flutter**. Unlike traditional digital clocks, this utility visualizes the passage of time through a rising water level, turning time management into a relaxing, zen-like experience.

## 🌟 Key Features
- **Intuitive Visual Progress:** Watch the water level rise from a faucet to represent elapsed time and percentage markers.
- **Dynamic Themes:** Highly customizable theme colors and full **Dark Mode** support to match your mood or environment.
- **Background Synchronization:** Seamlessly synchronized background video and elegant gradients for a polished aesthetic.
- **Flexible Duration:** Easily set countdowns up to **999 minutes** using a streamlined 3-digit picker.
- **Reliable Alerts:** Integrated system notifications with customizable sounds and vibrations that work even in the background.
- **Global Support:** Fully localized in **50+ languages**.

## 💡 Best For...
- **Focus & Productivity:** A more peaceful alternative to the Pomodoro technique.
- **Education:** Helping children grasp the concept of time through visual progress.
- **Meditation & Yoga:** Managing sessions without the stress of ticking clocks.
- **Kitchen Use:** Making the wait for a meal a moment of calm.

## 🛠 Technical Overview
- **Framework:** Flutter
- **Language:** Dart
- **Logic:** Custom painting for fluid level animations and synchronized background rendering. Includes robust background task handling for timer reliability.

*Note: This repository contains the core logic and source code. Assets (images, video loops, and localization files) are excluded to protect intellectual property.*
